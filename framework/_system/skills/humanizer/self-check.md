# humanizer - pre-ship self-check

- [ ] Voice committed: one wrench mode is firing (or `mirror` if samples were supplied), not AI-average default tone.
- [ ] Zero Tier-1 words (delve, tapestry, pivotal, testament, meticulous, nuanced, multifaceted, embark, spearhead, bolster, garner, interplay, realm, intricate, landscape-abstract, underscore-verb).
- [ ] Tier-2 words replaced or down to incidental use; Latinate verbs swapped for Anglo-Saxon (use/help/cut/lift/build).
- [ ] No adverb-intensifiers (really, just, simply, actually, genuinely, truly, fundamentally) and no hedge softeners (it could be argued, arguably, you may want to).
- [ ] No rule-of-three padding; no 'not X but Y' / 'not only X but also Y' pivots; no copula avoidance (serves as / acts as / represents).
- [ ] Sentence length varies hard — no run of 3+ same-length sentences; at least one deliberate fragment and at least one one-line paragraph for contrast.
- [ ] Burstiness reads high (≥~0.72 feel): a long clausey sentence is followed by a short punch somewhere in every section.
- [ ] why→how→what ordering; topic-sentence restatements cut; first-10% throat-clearing and all signposting removed.
- [ ] Formal transitions capped at ~2 per section; no clustered Furthermore/Moreover/Additionally; no secondary crutch quietly replacing the one you killed.
- [ ] Specificity present: real numbers, proper nouns, dates, error strings stand where abstractions used to be; no 'experts say' / vague attribution survives.
- [ ] Show-don't-tell holds: no broad sweeping emotional statements that say nothing; every importance claim names the specific implication.
- [ ] A position is taken; both-sides hedging gone; exactly one earned caveat per piece (or per section for long-form), placed where the argument is weakest.
- [ ] At least one human thinking-trace present (mid-thought correction, parenthetical admission, or honest 'I was wrong about X').
- [ ] At least 30% of paragraphs do NOT land on a tidy takeaway; no moral wrap-up, no 'the future looks bright,' no 'ultimately it depends.'
- [ ] Em-dashes ≤ ~1–2 per 500 words; no semicolons reaching for polish; straight quotes only; no smart/curly quotes.
- [ ] No mechanical bolding, no '**Term:** explanation' inline-header bullets, sentence-case headings, no emoji, no zero-width Unicode.
- [ ] Contractions present where speech would use them; no chatbot artifacts ('great question,' 'hope this helps,' knowledge-cutoff disclaimers).
- [ ] Self-critique meta-pass was run and its flagged sentences were rewritten — not skipped.
- [ ] Fact-diff passed: every number, name, date, and quote matches the pre-rewrite snapshot exactly.
- [ ] Read-aloud test passed (doesn't sound like a news anchor reading a textbook); five-dimension score ≥ ~35/50; left deliberately slightly uneven, not buffed smooth.
- [ ] No detector-evasion gimmickry: no injected typos, fake slang, or 'chaos' — every change makes the prose better to a human editor.
